My Pets

Growing up, my family has always had resident cats, so i've gotten to experience many different cat personalities. I can say withough a doubt that each of my fur babies have had their own unique qualities and quirks, proving no two cats are alike.

Luna

Luna is the sweetest little kitten that you will ever meet. Her favorite activities include sun bathing, playing in the kitchen and grooming her dad. She loves eating pizza, butter, and cream cheese, whenever she gets the chance. Her best friends are Pew and Grumpkins. She loves watching hockey games on tv.

Fun Facts

  • Breed: Khao Mannee
  • Hair Color: White
  • Eye Color: Green
  • Birthday: November 4, 2014
  • Hogwarts House: Slytherin

Pepé Le Pew

Pew is the silliest little kitten that you will ever meet. Her favorite activities include sun bathing, playing with electronic toys, and playing with Grumpkins. She loves Luna dearly and will cuddle with her whenever she gets the chance. She is incredibly shy with strangers, but loves her parents and granparents dearly. She loves watching nature documentaries on tv.

Fun Facts

  • Breed: Nebelung
  • Hair Color: Gray
  • Eye Color: Yellow-Green
  • Birthday: May 3, 2017
  • Hogwarts House: Hufflepuff

Grumpkins

Grumpkins is the sweetest little kitten that you will ever meet. Her favorite activities include following Pew around, rolling around the floor and playing with her toys. She loves treats, especially the gravy ones.
